About the Group 研究團隊簡介

We are part of the NYCU Institute of Physics in Hsinchu, Taiwan. Our mission is to advance non‑perturbative quantum field theory using lattice methods and high‑performance computing, and to mentor early‑career scientists for international research careers.
本團隊隸屬於國立陽明交通大學物理研究所，致力以格點方法與高效能運算推進非微擾量子場論，培育具國際競爭力之年輕研究者。
